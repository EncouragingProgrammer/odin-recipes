Heather's Lasagna

Homepage

Heather's amazing lasagna recipe is simple and delicous!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 28oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 can mushroom pieces & juice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese
  • grated mozzarella
  • box lasagna noodles
  • black pepper

Directions

  1. Brown the ground beef with the onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce, mushrooms, water, oregano, and pepper to taste.
  3. Simmer about 5 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix cottage cheese, egg, and parmesan.
  5. Layer in a 9x13 pan, 1/3 meat sauce, noodles, 1/3 meat sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, noodles, meat sauce, and sprinkle generously with grated mozzarella.
  6. Bake 45 minutes @ 375C covered with foil, uncover and bake for another 15 minutes.
  7. Alternatively, unbaked lasagna can be covered and frozen. Cooking from frozen add approximately 30 minutes additional cooking time.
