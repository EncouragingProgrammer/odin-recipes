Spaetzle

Homepage

Simple delicious German egg noodle that goes great with roast beef and gravy.

Ingredients

  • 4-1/2 cups white flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients with water as needed to form a thick, sticky consistency. Mix vigorously until smooth.
  2. Boil water, string into boiling water & stir gently. Allow water to come to a boil, then remove when noodles float on top.
